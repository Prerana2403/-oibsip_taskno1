Perfume is a story in odour,
sometimes poetry in memory.

We do know that people were making perfume around 4000 years ago in China, Egypt, India and the Middle East.
And from ancient Mesopotamian documents, we know that the world's first chemist was a woman called Tapputi,
who mixed up fragrances from natural flowers and oils.
